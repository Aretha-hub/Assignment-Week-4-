About

Welcome to Maria Kotze Gardens, where we seamlessly merge function and beauty to redefine outdoor spaces. Our landscapes are crafted with a keen understanding of botanical intricacies, creating gardens that beckon as extra living rooms. Guided by principles of proportion, color, balance, and texture, we curate environments that celebrate both indigenous and exotic plants. At Maria Kotze Gardens, we transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary retreats, harmonizing creativity and horticultural expertise to elevate outdoor living. Experience the transformative power of nature with us, where every corner becomes a living work of art.

Services

At Maria Kotze Gardens, our comprehensive range of services is tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From meticulous garden design and installation to expert plant selection and maintenance, we provide end-to-end landscaping solutions. Our skilled team is adept at creating everything from serene retreats to vibrant social spaces, ensuring that each project reflects the unique vision and lifestyle of our clients.
